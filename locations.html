Ceremony

Located in the heart of Old San Juan, the San Juan Bautista Cathedral is a magnificent testament to the rich history and architectural splendor of Puerto Rico. This Roman Catholic cathedral, built in 1540, has witnessed centuries of love stories, which makes it the perfect backdrop for our romantic celebration. As you walk through its grand entrance, you'll be transported back in time, surrounded by the warm glow of stained glass windows and the intricate details of its Baroque facade.

As we exchange our vows beneath the cathedral's soaring arches, we invite you to share in the beauty and enchantment of this historic site. The San Juan Bautista Cathedral offers a unique blend of old-world charm and modern elegance that will create an unforgettable experience for our wedding ceremony. Join us as we begin our journey together in this breathtaking setting, a testament to the enduring power of love and the magic of Old San Juan.

Reception

Snuggled in the picturesque city of Ponce, the Meliá Century Hotel offers an exquisite blend of historic charm and contemporary luxury. This enchanting hotel from the late 19th century has been meticulously restored to preserve its original grandeur while offering the finest modern amenities. You'll be greeted by ornate crystal chandeliers and gleaming marble floors as you enter the hotel's elegant ballroom, while the floor-to-ceiling windows reveal a stunning city view.

The Meliá Century Hotel's romantic atmosphere will provide the perfect ambiance for our wedding reception. As the sun sets, we'll move the celebration to the hotel's outdoor terrace, where you can dance beneath the stars and enjoy the cool ocean breeze. The Meliá Century Hotel is more than just a venue; it's a place where memories are made, and we can't wait to share this special day with you in a genuinely ubiquitous location.
