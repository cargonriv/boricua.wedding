Frequently Asked Questions

What should I wear to the wedding?

The dress code for the wedding is suggested by the locations in the Events tab of our wedding website. If you're still unsure, feel free to reach out to us for more guidance.

Can I bring a guest to the wedding?

The invitation should indicate whether or not you can bring a guest. If the invitation does not specify, it's best to reach out to us for clarification.

What time does the wedding start?

The start time of the wedding ceremony is indicated on the Locations tab of our wedding website. We recommend arriving at least 30 minutes before the ceremony start time.

Is there a gift registry for the wedding?

Yes, we have registered for gifts at several stores. You can find the details and links to our gift registry on our Registry page.

Can I bring my children to the wedding?

The invitation should indicate whether or not children are allowed to attend the wedding. If you're unsure, please reach out to us for clarification.
